Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 06, 2025

Welcome to MwNisH Translator. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data Collection

We do not store any text, voice data, or personal information on our servers. All translations are processed in real-time using third-party APIs (MyMemory) and are not saved by us.

2. Voice Data

Voice recognition is handled directly by your browser's Web Speech API. Your voice data is processed by your device or browser provider (e.g., Google) and is not sent to our servers.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. Your preferences (like language selection) may be stored temporarily in your local browser.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact Manish Ghimire.
